BMW M-sport cars traditionally include modified engines , transmissions , suspensions , interior trims , aerodynamics and exterior modifications to set them apart from their counterparts.

What does M8 mean?

The BMW M8 is the high performance version of the BMW 8 Series , marketed under the BMW M sub-brand . Typically powered by a high-performance engine , often a V8 or V12 . Offers exceptional horsepower and torque for exhilarating acceleration and top speeds . Advanced drivetrain and suspension systems for superior handling and stability. Features a aggressive and sleek design that enhances aerodynamics . Distinctive styling cues differentiate it from standard 8 Series models. Often includes unique M-specific elements such as larger air intakes, M badging, and quad exhaust pipes.Available in various body styles, including coupe, convertible, and gran coupe (four-door). Offers different performance levels under the M lineup, such as the M8 Competition with enhanced performance features.

About M8 Engine

The M8 is powered by a twin-turbo 4.4-liter V-8 that makes 617 horsepower and 749.77 newton-metre of torque with 4395 cubic-centimetres . This power enables rapid acceleration and high-speed performance. The M8 features advanced performance technologies such as BMW's M xDrive all-wheel-drive system, which helps optimize traction and handling in various driving conditions. The M8 typically comes with an advanced transmission system, such as an 8-speed M Steptronic automatic transmission, designed to deliver quick gear changes and smooth driving experience. While performance is a priority, BMW also incorporates technologies for improved fuel efficiency where possible, though exact figures can vary based on driving conditions and engine configuration.

About M8 luxury

The M8 features high-quality materials throughout its interior, including fine leather upholstery, genuine wood or carbon fiber trim and aluminum accents. These materials are meticulously crafted to enhance comfort and aesthetic appeal. The interior of the M8 is designed with comfort in mind, offering features such as multi-way power-adjustable seats with heating, ventilation, and massage functions. Advanced climate control systems, ambient lighting, and customizable driving modes further enhance the luxury experience. The M8 is equipped with BMW’s latest iDrive infotainment system, which includes a high-resolution touchscreen display, voice commands, navigation, smartphone integration, cruise control, lane departure warning, parking assistance and surround-view cameras, enhancing convenience and safety.
